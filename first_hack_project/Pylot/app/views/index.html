About

We are excited to have been selected as the focus project for the inaugural #FirstHack event and look forward to launching our full site in Q4 2016. Our site will be driven by the community, and while we are starting in the North Texas area, we see potential to rapidly begin to support communities nation-wide.

Bringing the Community Together... for YOU! We are creating a centralized and update-to-date platform that allows those impacted by HIV (whether they are the patient, a parent, sibling or friend) an easily navigable site to find resources that will save their lives.
