ABOUT US.

Bringing the Community Together... for YOU

We are creating a centralized and update-to-date platform that allows those impacted by HIV (whether they are the patient, a parent, sibling or friend) an easily navigable site to find resources that will save their lives.

We are excited to have been selected as the focus project for the inaugural #FirstHack event and look forward to launching our full site in Q4 2016.

Our site will be driven by the community, and while we are starting in the North Texas area, we see potential to rapidly begin to support communities nation-wide.

Support Services.

People often need a helping hand - but to get that help they need to know who to call and what they will need. We will provide up-to-date information on services available (food, shelter, counselling, healthcare, etc.) including detailed service descriptions, and comprehensive requirements for servcies - all available in a centralized location.

Community Building

Education and participation are the key to survival for those impacted by HIV - we will build a community that will support its members. Our goal is not to build a static site, but rather an online community where memebers can contribute ideas and information, as well as ask questions, all in a safe environment.

Support We're here for you.

Finding support is critical - we will provide it in an anonymous, secure environment that places the need of our members for privacy above all else.
